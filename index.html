Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get paid?

Earnings are credited to your wallet after task approval. You can withdraw to M-Pesa anytime. Minimum withdrawal is KES 50 for earnings wallet.

What is the difference between packages?

Starter package (KES 327) offers poetry tasks only with 4 task limit. Expert package (KES 427) includes both poetry and short stories with 6 task limit and higher earning potential.

How long does task approval take?

Tasks are typically reviewed within 24-48 hours. You'll receive a notification once approved and earnings will be credited to your wallet.

Can I request a refund?

Yes, refund requests can be submitted through the support section in your dashboard within 7 days of payment.
